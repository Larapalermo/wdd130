Argentina

Argentina is a country with many beautiful landscapes and a particular culture. The favorite sport of most Argentines

is soccer, we won three world cups. It is also a tradition to always drink mate, and to eat asado every Sunday with the

family or on special occasions, and to play "Truco" (a card game similar to Poker). Argentines eat a lot. Empanadas, asado

and pastas are the most common meals. Dulce de Leche is the richest and most consumed sweet in Argentina, we use it for many

desserts, the most common is the Chocotorta. In Argentina we speak very loudly and with an accent similar to Italy, we still

use "vos" to say "you" and the most famous slang is "che" to refer to another person in a friendly way.

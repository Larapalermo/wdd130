Overview

Purpose

We are Rafting Sella! Our goal is to encourage, teach and guide adventurers, families and groups of friends so that they can enjoy this beautiful sport such as rafting. We know you will find adventure, fun and enjoy the beautiful landscapes and the service we offer to everyone! On our website you will find available dates, our contacts and incredible prices!

Audience

As a company we help all types of people to enjoy Rafting, our service is intended for adventurers, families, groups of friends, special events, conventions, incentive trips, educational and to those people and organizations interested in new adventures, beautiful landscapes and quality moments with their loved ones. This service is also perfect for people with a less stable financial situation, because our prices are affordable and there are many payment options.
